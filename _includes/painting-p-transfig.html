TOGGLE WORK DESCRIPTION

Transfiguration

2015-16

PAINTING

Transfiguration was inspired by alchemy, and its close relationship to sculpture. In alchemy, one is able to transform one material into another, utilizing some sort of formal trade-off or pairing. In sculpture, objects are transformed instantly all the time, whether it moves its place of showing, or it metamorphoses during its conceptual stages.

More specifically, this work looks at the changes an object undergoes when it is being developed. Prototype materials like unfired clay, cardboard mockups, and a final piece of ceramic are used as a metaphor showcasing an objects playfulness into a more rigid, durable format.
