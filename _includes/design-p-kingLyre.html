King & Lyre | Visual Arts Publication

DESIGN

CREATOR | EDITOR | ART DIRECTOR

This publication is serves as a vehicle that uses design as a means of illustrating new points of political discourse. This project was designed to strengthen the understanding of contextual frameworks within text and imagery, as well as put together an efficient means of undergoing thorough research, design, and execution.

The most important responsibility is to produce, foster, and maintain relationships with vendors, artists, writers, and consumers that are needed in the production of a great product.

King & Lyre originated from a desire to gather collaborators and contemporaries from across different media into one portable space. In a printed form, aesthetic challenges and unique forms of communication can be experimented with. In a digital format, a new way of cataloguing language and the importance of historical context can be explored.
