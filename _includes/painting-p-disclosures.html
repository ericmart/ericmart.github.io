TOGGLE WORK DESCRIPTION

Disclosures (Series)

2016

PAINTING

Disclosures is a series of work that takes advantage of the building materials the are used for packing goods, marketing goods, or protecting goods. Materials like styrofoam and acrylic displays are rich in context that allow a playful seriousness when used within a painting.

The conversation should not surround itself with debate of painting versus sculpture, however, the inquiry does lie in how painting, as a form, acts as the same type of protector, packer, and marketer of ideas.
