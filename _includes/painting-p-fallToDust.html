TOGGLE WORK DESCRIPTION

Fall To Dust I & II

2015-16

PAINTING

History is a common thread throughout all of my work, especially in the Fall To Dust works. Fall To Dust I consists of sifting excavated dirt onto a form to leave a tracing of its shape. The work is incased within a frame built in a similar vein to an archaeological artifact or excavation site.

In Fall To Dust II, the same painting is lifted and hung onto a wall, allowing the paper to fall as it may, displacing the tracing and creating a new work.
