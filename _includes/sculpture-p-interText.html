TOGGLE WORK DESCRIPTION

Inter-Text-You-Ability

2015-16

SCULPTURE

Inter-Text-You-Ability is a multi-faceted sculpture that encompasses an installation, a poem, and a video work. This large endeavor pushes the limit of physicality and endurance of the artwork, and the artist making it. The installation features an art exhibition staged within itself, showcasing a small collection of commissioned ready-made objects, along with a piece from the artist as a homage to Constantin Brâncuși.
