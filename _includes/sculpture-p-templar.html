TOGGLE WORK DESCRIPTION

Templar

2015-16

SCULPTURE

Language is a powerful tool in art, and in the Templar piece, the coded nature of the title gives weight to the formal and material qualities of the work itself. The basic building materials chosen are materials man has used throughout history to build nations, forms, and objects that still dominate our way of living. In addition are found materials, like a log from the Santa Monica mountains which holds an acrylic platform, which adorns a drop of the artist's blood.
