Design What? | A Graphic Designer's Manual

DESIGN

EDITOR | GRAPHIC DESIGNER

Design What? is a thesis publication aimed at demonstrating a focused approach to information, aesthetics and audience. The goal of this project was to create a Graphic Design instruction manual that both an experienced designer, or a curious patron could understand easily in addition to learn valuable information and implementation.

Dimensions: 4.5" x 6.5" Saddle-Stitched Iconography found on thenounproject.com
