TOGGLE WORK DESCRIPTION

Let Me Help You

2015

PAINTING

Let Me Help You is a work which asks the viewer to contemplate the various modes of production in art, the body's relationship to the viewed work, and if one may be imaginative, the implications of self-referential art.

As part of an internal investigation with history, and how history can be embedded or removed from a work, Let Me Help You repurposes the building materials from sculptures and paintings of the past. Adorned on an acrylic pedestal high above the viewer sits a 3-d printed abstract form with a mirror pointed at it.
